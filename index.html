Advertising!

Context

Advertising is a marketing tactic. It is a way to promote your products or services, it informs people about your product so if someone saw your advertisement and they’re interested in your product then they’ll buy it. Advertising is very important if you want people to find out about your product or your service without advertising it would be really hard for you to inform other. There are two ways of advertising, you can advertise your product physically or virtually. There are many apps that can help you create or give out ads on the internet. There are also a lot of technologies that can help you advertise your product.
